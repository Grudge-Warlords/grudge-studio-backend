Our primary domain is grudge-studio.com and identity services are hosted at id.grudge-studio.com.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Account management — creating and maintaining your Grudge ID
  • Game operation — running game logic, storing progress, matchmaking
  • Wallet services — auto-creating server-side Solana wallets, tracking GBUX balances, minting NFTs
  • Security — rate limiting, ban enforcement, fraud prevention
  • Discord bot — responding to slash commands with your game data
  • Analytics — anonymous aggregate metrics (player counts, feature usage) — not sold to third parties
  • Communications — critical security notices (we don't send marketing email without consent)

4. Data Sharing

We share your data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Puter.com — our identity infrastructure. Puter stores your Puter UUID and username as part of Grudge ID authentication.
  • Discord — when you use Discord OAuth or our Discord bot, Discord's own privacy policy applies to data on their platform.
  • Crossmint — for compressed NFT minting. We share your wallet address and character metadata.
  • Helius / Solana RPC — blockchain data queries. These are public blockchain interactions.
  • Cloudflare — all traffic passes through Cloudflare's network for DDoS protection and CDN.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose data if required by valid law enforcement requests.

We never sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

5. Blockchain Data

On-chain transactions (GBUX transfers, NFT mints, wallet activity) are public by nature of the Solana blockchain. Once written on-chain, this data cannot be deleted. Your wallet public key is visible to anyone who queries the blockchain.

We store your wallet public key in our database to link it to your Grudge ID, but we never store private keys — those are derived on our secure server using your account's HD wallet index and are only accessible through the wallet service.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts — data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts — most personal data deleted within 30 days. Anonymous game stats may be retained.
  • Log data — server logs retained for up to 30 days for security purposes
  • Blockchain data — permanent (blockchain transactions cannot be deleted)

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — update inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — receive your game data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us via Discord or at grudge-studio.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All passwords are hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plaintext)
  • All services run behind Cloudflare's DDoS protection and TLS 1.3
  • JWTs are signed with HS256 and expire after 7 days
  • Server-side wallets use HD derivation — private keys are never stored, only indices
  • Internal service communication uses shared secret keys, never exposed to clients
  • Rate limiting is enforced on all auth endpoints

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us immediately via Discord.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

We use:

  • grudge_sso — httpOnly cookie for cross-game single sign-on (7 day expiry)
  • grudge_auth_token — localStorage for your session JWT (managed by you)
  • Cloudflare cookies — standard Cloudflare security cookies (_cf_bm, cf_clearance)

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes
